Squid games

Seung Gi Hun

Gi-hun is a divorced and indebted chauffeur, former assembly worker, and an obsessed gambler.Gi-hun recognizes his faults and is willing to improve for his family. He joins the Squid Game thinking the money will help him be a better father for his ten-year-old daughter and save the life of his sickly mother.

Kang Sae-Byeok

She was a North Korean defector, and fled the country with her parents and younger brother, leading to her father's death and her mother getting caught. A pickpocket, Sae-byeok entered the Squid Game to get enough money to reunite her family.

Jang Deok-su

At some point in his life, Deok-su fell into bad standings with his criminal boss in Incheon and accumulates large gambling debts with loan sharks from the Philippines. He agrees to enter the Squid Game to escape those pursuing him and to win enough money to pay his debts. Throughout the games, he was known to be aggressive and hostile towards other players.
